What We Do

Our goal is to foster an interest in Astronomy and Astrophysics through stargazing events and competitions. We offer instruction in the various fields of Astronomy and Astrophysics.

Compete in the Astronomy and Astrophysics Olympiad

The International Olympiad for Astronomy and Astrophysics (IOAA) is an annual high school science competition in which over forty countries participate

Learn More

Hold Study Sessions for the Astronomy and Astrophysics Olympiad

Hold study sessions for the Astronomy and Astrophysics Olympiad, with full guidance and support from Astronomy Club officers.

Stargazing Events

Learn how to use a telescope at monthly stargazing events at the Monte Bello Open Space Preserve, where you can get extravagant views of the universe.

Learn More

News and Discussions

Talk about interesting topics in Astronomy and Astrophysics and get the latest news on new discoveries and observations.
